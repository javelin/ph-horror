## Scan-order warning

The two batches were not captured in the same direction. They must not be
interleaved in their raw PDF order.

The odd-page batch proceeds generally from the beginning of the body toward the
end. In contrast, the substantive even-page run begins near the end of the
book: `even-015` is printed page 128 and `even-016` is printed page 126. Later
even scans move toward lower page numbers. Repeated, blank, and damaged
captures interrupt both runs, so printed numbers must still be verified
individually.

The source PDFs are not committed to this repository. The identifiers
`odd-NNN` and `even-NNN` refer to their one-based PDF scan positions.
